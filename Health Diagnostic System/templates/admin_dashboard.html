How auto-assignment works

When a patient completes a diagnosis, the AI recommends a doctor specialty (e.g. Cardiologist).

MediAI then finds the doctor of that specialty with the fewest active consultations and auto-assigns the case.

If no matching specialty is found, the doctor with the lightest load overall is selected.

Tip: Add at least one doctor per specialty for best results.

Create Lab Technician Account
Lab tech workflow

When a doctor orders lab tests for a patient, the system auto-assigns the order to the lab technician with the fewest pending tests.

Lab techs log in, see their pending orders, fill in results (value, unit, normal range, abnormal flag), and submit.

The doctor immediately sees the results in the consultation view.
